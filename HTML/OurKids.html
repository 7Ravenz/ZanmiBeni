Child of the week / Enfant de la semaine - Carl

Carl is 9 years old and in the second grade. He loves school, Power Rangers, soccer and poutine. Carl traveled to Canada at the age of 4 to receive treatment at the Shriner’s Hospital for Children in Montreal. As a result he now runs, plays and dances with his crutches. Carl placed first in his grade last year, and he would like to become a doctor like Dr. Hamdy, Peter, Connor and Maggie. Carl has a bright future in front of him.
